Our manifesto

Affinity Collective is for
the most
passionate people
in the world at what they do.
In fact, if you are reading this,
you are likely one
of the best
in the world at what you do.

We believe that Web3 is exponentially bigger than
Web2 and will turn the world upside down.

“Web 3.0 is the biggest economic
opportunity of our lifetime.”

Eric Pulier, Web 3.0 Advocate and
creator of the 1st NFT in 2015

While we think making money is cool and
encourage that,
we are not a group of flippers
who are just looking at this space as a get
rich
quick scheme. We are individuals who are here
for the long term.

Some of us have been through many/all of the
Crypto bear markets,
made and lost fortunes
and made them back again, but more
importantly just absolutely love the space.

While Web3 and NFTs are our North Star,
these aren't the only things
Affinity Collective
is about. What we are creating is much more
than that.

We are bringing people together who are
truly passionate
(but also world-class) at
what they do. You bring some incredible
value
to the world, and we are creating a
safe environment to share, learn,
inspire and
collaborate in big ways with fellow members

We are family

Deep conversations are not encouraged, they
are mandatory.
We aren't creating a casual
networking group to make some friends.
We
are not your friends, we are your family.

Affinity Collective builds your network with
some of the most
innovative, collaborative, and
brilliant minds in Web3. We want people
in
Affinity Collective that love and embrace the
ethos
of the decentralized, open, Web3 world.

How are you going to
use web 3.0,
Blockchain, NFT
to make the world a better
place?
Together we will find out.
Let’s play a bigger game.
Web 3.0 deserves more.

To sum it up:

Affinity represents die-hard passion and
commitment to
bettering the world,
collaborating and winning together. A big
part of
Affinity Collective is about finding
ways to collaborate and win with your
fellow members. Sharing the unique value
that you bring to business and
to this new
world we are creating together.

Sincerely, Affinity Collective

Affinity Collective is a
private web 3.0 community
